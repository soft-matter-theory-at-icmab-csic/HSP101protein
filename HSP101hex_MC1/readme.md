Structures showing the interaction of MC1 with an hexamer of HSP101 inferred from alignment of monomer HSP101 - MC1 structures:
This folder contains an equilibrated structure of HSP101 hexamer and alligned possible binding complex of hsp101-MC1 predicted by AF and megadock.
