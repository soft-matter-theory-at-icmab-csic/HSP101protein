### Structures showing the interaction of MC1 with an hexamer of HSP101 inferred from alignment of monomer HSP101 - MC1 structures:

This folder contains an refined-structure of HSP101 hexamer before the 100-ns production-run and alligned possible binding complexes of hsp101-MC1 as predicted by MD refinement of binding modes predicted by AF and megadock. 
Opening these four pdb files will present you the binding sites of MC1 to the haxamer structure.
