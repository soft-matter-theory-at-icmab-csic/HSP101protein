### Structures showing the interaction of MC1 with an hexamer of HSP101 inferred from alignment of monomer HSP101 - MC1 structures:

This folder contains a 100-ns production-run–equilibrated structure of the HSP101 hexamer, along with aligned candidate HSP101–MC1 binding complexes generated through MD-refined binding modes predicted by AlphaFold and MEGADOCK.
Opening the four provided PDB files will allow you to visualize the potential MC1 binding sites on the hexamer structure.
