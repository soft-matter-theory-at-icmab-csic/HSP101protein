## Simulations of binding of MC1 to HSP101

Results from Molecular Dynamics simulations of a single HSP101 protein interacting with a MC1 protein from different initial conditions:

- Initial condition generated by AlphaFold 3.
- Initial conditions generated by docking calculations (best pose and second best pose) using Megadock https://bmcresnotes.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s13104-023-06505-w

We provide the pdb and psf files of the initial structures (generated by AlphaFold or docking) and the final structures after refinement by Molecular Dynamics.

The *comparison* folder compares the three obtained structures after aligment
