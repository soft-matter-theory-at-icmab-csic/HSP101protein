## Simulations binding of MC1 to HSP101

Results from Molecular Dynamics simulations of a single HSP101 protein intracting with a MC1 protein from different initial conditions:

- Initial condition generated by AlphaFold 
- Initial conditions generated by docking calculations (poses 1 and 2) using Megadock https://bmcresnotes.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s13104-023-06505-w

We provide the pdb and psf files of the structures and images of the results.
